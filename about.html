About Us

INFOTECH INFORMATION

Since its formation, Infotech Maldives Pte Ltd has pioneered the way in solving clients' real world IT issues with quality of service and sound technology.

Infotech is an information communications technology company that designs systems and solutions.During the first few years of its inception, the company focused on software development and providing customized software solutions to private businesses and government institutions in the country.

We are also specializing in creating enterprise content management, business process automation, workflow, records management, document imaging and webform software. The Infotech portfolio of expertise includes WindowsOS, LAN&WAN Network, Cable Telephone and CCTV Camera and their integration and maintainance within an enterprise.

Our History

Infotech (Maldives) Pvt Ltd which was formed in 1993 is specializes in working with many technologies and integration with other technologies and systems are a key capability of our company. We have earned a reputation for systems design and developing user friendly software. Today many reputed businesses in Maldives rely on our Consultancy, solutions and support for their IT investments.

As the IT industry in the Maldives picked up pace, the importance of providing total solutions grew. In keeping with the pulse of the emerging market, the company ventured into sourcing hardware and other peripherals. Today infotech has diversified into many market segments and is a key player in providing solutions in these segments.We have evolved our services from basic networking services to provide enterprise grade solutions.

Over the past two decades, the company has offered a wide range of pioneering products, ground breaking services and has continued to invest in its strengths to provide effective, efficient and reliable solutions to meet the demand of the dynamic IT and info-comms market in the Maldives.
